Ladies and gentlemen, much of what you’ve seen may be termed as illusions or entertaining trifles. Alas…I cannot claim this next feat as illusion. Watch carefully. You will see no trickery, for no trickery is employed. Merely a technique familiar to certain citizens of the Orient and various holy men of the Himalayas. Indeed, many of you may be familiar with this technique, but for those of you who are not, do not be alarmed. What you’re about to see is considered safe.
